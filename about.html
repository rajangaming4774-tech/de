About HapiWelkin

Founded in 2014, we're on a mission to build a world where every child grows up understanding their talent, career, and passion.

Our Story

If We Want to Change the World, We Must Start from Schools

At HapiWelkin Creative Institute, we believe that happiness, creativity, and innovation are the key elements of a successful and fulfilling life. Our mission is to empower students, professionals, and individuals through experiential learning, creative education, and emotional well-being programs.

We are committed to spreading happiness, enhancing creativity, and developing essential life skills that will shape a brighter future for individuals and the nation.

We strongly believe that children are the future, and what we feed their minds today will create the world of tomorrow. However, in current education systems, there is a lack of clarity about happiness, career, and creativity. Students often struggle to understand what they are good at, what career path suits them, or what makes them happy.

Our mission is to fill this gap — transforming students today, transforming the world tomorrow.

🌟

Since 2014

A decade of transforming education across Tamil Nadu with happiness, creativity, and purpose.

Our Vision

  • A world where every child grows up understanding their talent, career, and passion
  • Every student knows their strengths and does what they love
  • Students stay mindful and present in their daily lives
  • Young minds think creatively and solve problems innovatively
  • Every individual contributes positively to society and lives a happy, meaningful life

Our Mission

  • Empower students, teachers, professionals, and individuals through creative education
  • Provide career guidance and help discover hidden talents
  • Promote well-being and happiness development
  • Enhance creativity through right-brain training and experiential learning
  • Build a growth mindset and help lead purpose-driven lives
  • Gain career clarity through structured discovery programs
