School Programs

Four pillars of creative education designed to help every student discover their talent, happiness, and career path.

Happiness Education

Hapi Program

The Hapi Program focuses on emotional well-being, mindfulness, and positivity. It helps students understand what truly makes them happy, manage stress, and build resilience for life.

Key Benefits

😊

Emotional Intelligence

Students learn to understand and manage their own emotions and empathize with others.

🧘

Mindfulness

Techniques for staying present, focused, and calm even during stressful times like exams.

🌱

Growth Mindset

Fostering a positive approach to challenges and a love for continuous learning.

🤝

Positive Relationships

Building strong, supportive connections with peers, teachers, and family.

Program Details

Target AudienceGrades 1 to 12
FormatWeekly Sessions
DeliveryIn-School
Focus AreaEmotional Well-being

Creativity Development

Crea Program

Crea focuses on right-brain training and creative expression. Through art, design, and innovative thinking exercises, students learn to unlock their imagination and approach problems creatively.

Key Benefits

🎨

Creative Expression

Allowing students to communicate their thoughts and ideas through various artistic mediums.

🧠

Right-Brain Activation

Exercises specifically designed to stimulate the right hemisphere of the brain.

💡

Innovative Thinking

Encouraging out-of-the-box solutions and novel approaches to standard problems.

🛠️

Hands-on Making

Turning abstract ideas into tangible creations, building confidence in their abilities.

Program Details

Target AudienceGrades 1 to 12
FormatWeekly Workshops
DeliveryIn-School
Focus AreaCreative Skills

Career Discovery

Wami Program

Wami ("Who Am I") is designed to help students discover their strengths, passions, and ideal career directions. It provides clarity and purpose, helping students make informed choices about their future.

Key Benefits

🔍

Self-Discovery

Assessments and activities to help students identify their natural talents and interests.

🧭

Career Mapping

Exploring various career paths aligned with individual strengths and market trends.

🎯

Goal Setting

Learning how to set achievable short-term and long-term academic and career goals.

💼

Future Readiness

Developing the essential soft skills required for success in any future career.

Program Details

Target AudienceGrades 8 to 12
FormatSeminars & Counseling
DeliveryIn-School / Online
Focus AreaCareer Clarity

Design Entrance Coaching

DEEP Program

DEEP provides specialized coaching for students aspiring to clear prestigious design entrance exams (like NID, NIFT, UCEED, CEED, NATA). We prepare them with the skills and portfolio needed for a career in design.

Key Benefits

✏️

Sketching Skills

Intensive training in observation, perspective, human anatomy, and memory drawing.

🧠

Aptitude & Logic

Preparation for visual, spatial, and analytical reasoning sections of entrance exams.

📁

Portfolio Building

Guidance on creating a strong, professional design portfolio to impress examiners.

🎙️

Interview Prep

Mock interviews and studio test preparation for final admission rounds.

Program Details

Target AudienceGrades 11 & 12
FormatIntensive Coaching
DeliveryCenter / Online
Focus AreaNID, NIFT, UCEED
